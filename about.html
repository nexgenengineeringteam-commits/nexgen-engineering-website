NexGen Engineering is led by Hamed Jafaripour, a senior structural and civil engineer with more than 18 years of combined international and Australian engineering practice, including over 9 years of local Australian project delivery experience.

Hamed has delivered and coordinated structural, civil drainage, BIM documentation and construction-stage support across more than 107 documented projects — spanning residential, commercial, education, childcare, warehouse, industrial, mining-related and infrastructure-linked sectors.

Before establishing NexGen Engineering, Hamed held senior engineering and BIM coordination roles within Australian consulting environments, contributing to projects across Victoria, Western Australia and Queensland. International experience includes multi-storey reinforced concrete structures (up to 13 storeys), deep excavation systems, performance-based design, BIM management and research-driven structural engineering.
