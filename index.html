[ 05 ]Inspection & Reporting

Staged inspection pathway.

A staged inspection and reporting framework matched to the level of risk, available information and required outcome. Scope and fees are confirmed by quotation, as inspection depth, reporting requirements and certification responsibilities vary by project.

STAGE 1

Preliminary Site Review

Single Option

Desktop & Visual Preliminary Review

Initial desktop review plus basic visual site assessment. Scope confirmation, risk flagging and preliminary observations to inform next steps.

STAGE 2

Detailed Assessment & Reporting

Option A

Standard Assessment

Comprehensive site inspection with photographic record, defect identification, compliance checking against drawings and standards, and written assessment report.

Option B

Formal Assessment Support

A more detailed engineering assessment suitable where the matter may involve builder disputes, insurance queries, compliance concerns or third-party review. Includes a structured report, evidence-based observations and professional engineering opinion within the agreed scope. Expert witness or VCAT-specific reporting can be separately scoped if required.

Option C

Premium Assessment + Level Survey

Full assessment as per Option A/B plus coordinated level survey, precise dimensional verification and enhanced documentation for complex or high-value projects.

STAGE 3

Rectification & Delivery Support

Option D

Mid-Level Rectification Concept

Conceptual rectification strategy with scope outline, feasibility assessment and preliminary cost guidance for builder/client review.

Option E

Detailed Rectification Documentation

Full rectification design package with structural drawings, specifications, construction notes and compliance documentation for builder implementation.

Option F

Full Delivery & Construction Support

Complete rectification from design through construction — including documentation, hold-point inspections, RFI responses, contractor coordination, photographic records and engineering certification support where applicable and within the agreed scope.

[ 07 ]BIM & Digital Systems

BIM-Led Digital Engineering Delivery.

NexGen delivers structural and civil engineering through BIM-first workflows — BIM is not treated as a presentation add-on; it is used to improve documentation clarity, discipline coordination, constructability review and information control.

We support Revit-based documentation, BIM Execution Plan input, Common Data Environment setup, model coordination, issue tracking, clash awareness and structured information workflows aligned with ISO 19650 principles where required. For firms looking to adopt BIM or improve existing workflows, NexGen provides setup, training and ongoing coordination support aligned with ISO 19650.

For Engineering Firms: If your practice wants to transition to BIM or improve your Revit/Navisworks workflow, NexGen can support BIM transition, Revit documentation setup, CDE planning, BEP preparation, model coordination workflows and practical training for project delivery teams.
